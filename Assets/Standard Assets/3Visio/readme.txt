"3Visio Shapes" is an Editor Extension for building planes with regular or special shapes in a simple, fast and intuitive mode.
The shapes may have from 3 to 40 sides, and may be normal or star or gear.
Everything is controlled by a simple menu that includes all the possible options.
To install "3Visio Shapes" simply load the package and under the menu "GameObject" you will find the submenu to access to 3Visio Shapes.
